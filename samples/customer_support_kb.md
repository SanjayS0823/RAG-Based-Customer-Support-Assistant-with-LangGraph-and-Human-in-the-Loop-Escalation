# Acme Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy
- Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase.
- Refund requests after 14 days are evaluated case by case.
- Digital subscription renewals are non-refundable once the billing cycle starts.

## Cancellation Policy
- Monthly plans can be canceled anytime from the account settings page.
- Cancellation is effective at the end of the current billing cycle.
- Annual plans can be canceled, but remaining months are not prorated.

## Shipping and Delivery
- Standard shipping ETA is 5 to 7 business days.
- Expedited shipping ETA is 1 to 2 business days.
- If a package is marked delivered but not received, customers should wait 24 hours before filing a lost package claim.

## Account Access Issues
- For failed login attempts, customers should reset their password using the forgot-password flow.
- Accounts are temporarily locked for 15 minutes after 5 failed attempts.
- If 2FA code delivery fails, users should verify phone network availability and retry after 2 minutes.

## Troubleshooting App Errors
- Users should clear cache and restart the app for session-related issues.
- Persistent crash issues should be escalated with device model, OS version, and timestamp.
- Payment-processing errors should be escalated when retrying after 10 minutes still fails.

## Escalation Rules
- Escalate to human support when the user explicitly asks for a human agent.
- Escalate when policy interpretation is ambiguous or legal-risk related.
- Escalate when technical issues persist after standard troubleshooting.
